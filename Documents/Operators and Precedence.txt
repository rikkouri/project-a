Operators and Precedence

* What are operators
    - Constructs that allow the manipulation of operands
* Types of operators
    - Arithmetic : +,-,*,/,%
    - Relational / comparative : >,<,==,!=,>=,<=
    - Assignment : +=, -=, *=, /=,%=,**=,//=
    - Logical : &&,||,!
    - Membership: in, not in
    - Identity: is, is not
